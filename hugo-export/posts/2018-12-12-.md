This year I stuck with Ubuntu/Debian derivatives as usual as I just can&#8217;t get the bad taste of SE Linux out of my mouth (looking at you Fedora, RHEL, Centos). Getting back to my stuck-in-the-mind, don&#8217;t-change-what-works mentality, the no-desktop, spartan approach that Ubuntu Unity and Gnome take were not good for my workflows. I like to see elements so a bit of clutter isn&#8217;t always a problem for me. But two of the options I considered, Pop OS and Elementary OS, appear to be Gnome-based or at least offer similar stay-out-of-you-way approaches to desktop elements.Â
